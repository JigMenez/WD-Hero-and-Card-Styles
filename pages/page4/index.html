Bulbasaur

The Seed Poekmon

Bulbasaur is one of the most beloved starter Pokémon in the Pokémon series. This Grass/Poison-type Pokémon is known for the plant bulb on its back, which grows into a large flower as it evolves. Bulbasaur is friendly and loyal, making it a favorite among trainers.
