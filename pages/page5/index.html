Hero 5

Rayquaza

The Sky Pokemon

Rayquaza is a legendary Dragon/Flying-type Pokémon from the Pokémon series. Dwelling in the ozone layer, Rayquaza is known for its ability to calm the raging conflicts between Kyogre and Groudon. Its majestic appearance and incredible power make it a revered figure in the Pokémon world.
