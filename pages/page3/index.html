Mordekaiser

The Iron Revenant

Mordekaiser is a formidable and malevolent figure in the game League of Legends. Once a brutal warlord, he now commands the dead as the Iron Revenant. Clad in dark, enchanted armor, Mordekaiser seeks to conquer and enslave the souls of the living and the dead alike.
